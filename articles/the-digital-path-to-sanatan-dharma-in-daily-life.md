# What is Sanatan Dharma?

Sanatan Dharma literally means **"The Eternal Way."** Unlike many modern religious systems, it is not limited to a single book, prophet, or doctrine.

Instead, it is a vast spiritual tradition built on scriptures such as:

- Vedas  
- Upanishads  
- Bhagavad Gita  
- Ramayana  
- Mahabharata  
- Puranas  

Sanatan Dharma teaches principles like:

- **Dharma** (righteous duty)  
- **Karma** (action and consequence)  
- **Moksha** (liberation)  
- **Bhakti** (devotion)  
- **Yoga** (union with the divine)  

For millions of Hindus across India and the world, these teachings guide everyday life.

But the challenge today is **accessibility and daily engagement**.
